EXAMPLE MEDICAL REPORT INPUTS
==============================

Example 1: Blood Test Report
------------------------------
LABORATORY REPORT

Patient: John Doe
Date: 2024-01-15

Complete Blood Count:
- Hemoglobin: 14.2 g/dL
- Red Blood Cells (RBC): 5.1 million/µL
- White Blood Cells (WBC): 7500 /µL
- Platelets: 280,000 /µL

Blood Chemistry:
- Glucose: 95 mg/dL
- Cholesterol: 185 mg/dL

Blood Pressure: 120/80 mmHg

All values are within normal ranges.

---
